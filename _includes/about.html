About Me

I am a cognitive neuroscientist studying adaptive memory in humans.
My research examines how changes in our internal states influence the ways in which we search for, process, and encode new information.

I was a Postdoctoral Associate working with Dr. Alison Adcock at Duke University, and as of Sept 2024, I have started a new position at the NUS Center for Sleep & Cognition!

For future updates, find me at my lab website!
